Credits

OpenSesame is a collaborative effort. Many thanks go out to Daniel Schreij, Jan Theeuwes, Edwin Dalmaijer, Lotje van der Linden, the NeuroDebian team, Andrea Epifani, Jarik den Hartog, Jonathan Grainger, Françoise Vitu, Eric Castet, everybody at the Department of Cognitive Psychology at the VU University Amsterdam, and everybody at the Laboratoire de Psychologie Cognitive at Aix-Marseille University.

OpenSesame could not exist withouth many third party software packages, such as PsychoPy Expyriment PyGame, NumPy, and, of course, Python.

The icon theme is a customized mixture of Faenza by Tiheum, Elementary by DanRabbit, and the Gnome icon theme.

Citation

Mathôt, S., Schreij, D., & Theeuwes, J. (2012). OpenSesame: a graphical experiment builder for the social sciences. Behavior Research Methods, 44(2), 314-324. DOI: 10.3758/s13428-011-0168-7 [Full text]
